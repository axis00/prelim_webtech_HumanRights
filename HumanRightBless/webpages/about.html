About Leonila Hill

Barangay Leonila Hill Hall is a located near at Evangelista Street, it can be easily spotted if you actually ride a jeepney vehicle going to Aurora Hill. Leonila Hill road is just around at the right of the road of Evangelista Street and from there, you are now entering the barangay.

Leonila hill is a part of the thirteen barangays of Aurora Hill. It belongs to District 9-5. The population of the barangay estimates to a number of 1,411 people and 346 Households.

Originally,Leonila Hill was a vacation place by the original homeowners from manila. The proximity of the subdivision to the city proper made it ideal for vacation homes, considering that it is not far from the central business area. The surrounding hills that are still lush and verdant that provides clean environment and nice view.

Leonila hill is a place where you can't get lost,if you ever get lost you can ask the locals to know your way or whom you are finding for. Just like going to baguio, the road is difficult because of turns and twisted curves but as you go through these roads you will see beautiful Top High Pine trees and enhale fresh air every morning. Beautiful mountain views full of several houses are also to be sought here.

A lot of regular houses such as bungalow, townhouses, mid-rise or high-rise residential building and condo units are in the barangay. But on the other hand, there is no place for living, business and production. Only Mini Mart, General Stores and Food stands are available for your needs.

Barangay Facilities

Multi-purpose Hall- is a open space for all activities and programs in the barangay such as Nutrition Month,Youth Activities,Sportsfest and Games,Fire and earthquake drills,and other barangay related actions.It can be used in state of calamities and natural disasters.

Barangay Hall- An office and facility for the local officials and a area for their work.A place for the people who has concerns,problems,and complaints inside the barangay.

Talipapa- A small public market near the barangay hall where you can buy products made from the barangay and other goods.

Day Care center-A facility used by pre-school children for studying,developing their skills,and educational purposes.

Children's Playground- a space that is open not only for children but for everybody to use for outdoor games and entertainment.

Barangay Programs

Monitoring of Pregnant and Lactating Mothers is a program for both the mother and the child that aims to put the health of the both of the to safety. This program is done any month of the year. It is managed by the Barangay Nutrition Council. Aside from that, the Nutrition Council also manage another program which is the Nutition Month Celebration: Supplemental Feeding Program which aims to improve the health status of malnourished children and to encourage their primary caregivers to provide decent and nutritious meals.

ALS ( Alternative Learning System ) has the objective to conduct skills training and education using the ALS provided by the DepEd and other NGO's for the OSY's (Out of School Youth) and other interested constituents. It is conducted for the whole year and is managed by Barangay Kagawad Marvin Ayodoc who is in charge for the Education and Youth.

City parade (opening of Panagbenga Festival) is also part of the barangay's programs. They are participating in the annual celebration of the city of Pines by presenting their performances. It is conducted every 1st of February.

Summer or Youth Camp is conducted by the barangay. This program aims to have a team building activity for the children ages 10 to 14 years old which includes parlor games, camping and seminars regarding health, nutrition and values formation. It is also managed by Brgy. Kagawad Ayodoc and is held every month of April.

Halloween trick or treat is done on the last week of October. They prepare small presents for about 100 children of the barangay. It aims to enhance the children's sense of camaraderie, confidence, and creativity. This activity also aims to put smile on children's faces.

Share a Joy Program is like the "Christmas Party" of the barangay. They prepare 100-120 gifts for children and provide games with cash prices taken from the funds of the barangay.

Most of the program of Leonila Hill were held at their Multi-Purpose Hall.
