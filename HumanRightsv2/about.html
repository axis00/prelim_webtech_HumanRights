Barangay History

1950,"Working Man's Village" is the name of Leonila Hill and it is still a part of the Aurora Hill.

1956, Leonila Hill is 2.2 Hectares owned by a local developer.The land was sold to the GSIS and became and subdivision named "Sunrise Subdivision".

1957,In the term of Carlos P. Garcia,the subdivision was renamed to "Leonila Hill" but was not still considered as a barangay.It was commemorated for the first lady,Leonila Garcia

In 1958's'and the further years,The city was divided into barangays.The upper part of leonila hill became west bayan park barangay and the lower protion of the subdivion became north bayan park barangay according to their locations.

Today,Leonila Hill is a part of baguio city.Like Most other Barangay,It is a cozy place to live in that offers an experience of simplicity,sincerity and consistency of the barangay.It displace the Ambience that you searching for,friendly neighbors and commities at your service and Clean and alluring climate and environment.

About Leonila Hill

Barangay Leonila Hill Hall is a located near at Evangelista Street,it can be easily spotted if you actually ride a jeepney vehicle going there.Leonila Hill Road is just around at the right of the road of Evangelista Street and from there,You are now entering the barangay.

Leonila hill is a part of the thirteen barangays of Aurora Hill.It belongs to District 9-5.The population of the barangay estimates to a number of 1,411 people and 346 Households.

Originally,Leonila Hill was a vacation place by the original homeowners from manila.The proximity of the subdivision to the city proper made it ideal for vacation hmes,considering that,it is not far from the central business area.the surrounding hills that are still lush and verdant that provides clean environment and nice view.

Leonila hill is a place where you can't get lost,if you ever get lost you can ask the locals to know your way or whom you are finding for. Just like going to baguio,The Road is difficult because of turns and twisted curves but as you go through this roads you will see beautiful Top High Pine trees and a Fresh air every morning and .Beautiful mountain views full of several houses are also to be be sought here.

A lot of regular houses such as bungalow,townhouses,mid-rise or high-rise residential building and condounits are in the barangay. But on the other hand,there is no place for living,business and production.Only Mini Mart,General Stores and Food stand are available for your needs.

Barangay Facilities

Multipurpose Hall- is a open space for all activities and programs in the barangay such as Nutrition Month,Youth Activities,Sportsfest and Games,Fire and earthquake drills,and other barangay related actions.It can be used in state of calamities and natural disasters.

Barangay Hall- An office and facility for the local officials and a area for their work.A place for the people who has concerns,problems,and complaints inside the barangay.

Talipapa- A small public market near the barangay hall where you can buy products made from the barangay and other goods.

Day Care center-A facility used by pre-school children for studying,developing their skills,and educational purposes.

Children's Playground- a space that is open not only for children but for everybody to use for outdoor games and entertainment.
