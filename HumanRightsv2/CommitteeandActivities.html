Barangay Committies and Programs

In barangay leonila Hill,there are different committies responsible to handle a work such as:

Committee on Finance/Appropriations,lead by BK Manuela D. Gayo

Responsibilities related to budgeting ,appropriation of funds for the payment of obligations,compensations of the personnel,taxation ordinances and monetary and fiscal matters.

Committee on Clean and Green,lead by BK Erna R. Fariñas

They are responsible for all works regarding the cleanliness and tidiness of the whole barangay.

They also encourage and promote constituents to keep their place and surroundings clean.

Committee on Programs and Socials,lead by BK Zenaida S. Torres

they are pledged to strengthen bonds between families in the barangay , women's welfare,abolishing child abuse, and supervising the barangay day care center.

Committee on Peace and Order,lead by BK Rogelio T. Basto Jr.

Responsible for maintaining public peace,security,integrity,and order of the barangay and command barangay tanods for public control and monitoring.

Committee on Public Works

All matters relative to infrastructural improvements such as planning,construction,and repair of the barangay roads,parks,buildings,drainage and gardens.

Committee on Health and Sanitation,lead by BK Narcisa FLor B. Belvis

they are in charge for programs and activities for health and cleanliness of the environment and the person such as sanitation,cleanliness of sewer pipes,immunization,vaccination for animals and personal hygiene.

Special Committees

Barangay Nutrition Council (BNC),PB Margarita Mariñas

responsible for coordinating nutrition related events and activities in the barangay.

Work with responsible committees for nutritional events and activities and implementation of programs.

Bids and Awards Committee (BAC),BK Marvin Ayodoc

Advertise and post the inivitation to bid through the PHILGEPS for infrastructure projects.

responsible for conducting evaluation and receiving bids.

Barangay Council for Protection of Children (BCPC), BK Zenaida Torres

Educate every children in barangay

encourage parents to perform their duties to their children to enhance positive parent child relationships.

Protect and assist abandoned or maltreated children.

Barangay Peace and Order Committee(BPOC) - BK Rogelio T. Basto Jr.

to Monitor and coordinated the implementaion of peace and order programs and projects in the barangay level.

Barangay Fitness and Sports Development Council(BFSDC),BK Marvin Ayodoc

To promote physical fitness and sports development among inhabitants of the barangay.

Promote a healthy lifestyle and physical fitness everyday for the good of the barangay.

Baranday Development Council (BDC),Margarita Fariñas

To mobilize people's participation in all local development efforts.

Prepare barangay development plans based on local requirements.

Violence Against Women and Children (VAWC), BK Zenaida Torres

Assist the victims who was abused,attacked,molested,discriminated and mistreated.

Role to arrest the abuser or the attacker and settle it to the enforcement of the PO's.

Make sure that any person who violated the human rights will be fined and disciplined.
